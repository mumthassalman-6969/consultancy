OUR STORY

Business Cafe started in the year 2017 with the aim of helping Small and Medium Businesses to get a proper structure and set up best practices in Business. Over the past few years, we have grown into a complete strategic Business and HR consulting firm with focus on SMEs and start-up entities. As the name suggests, Clout simply believes in ‘ínfluencing’ the businesses to adopt best strategies and practices in order to transform it to a world class organization..
